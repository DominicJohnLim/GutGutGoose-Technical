# Sellimonas intestinalis
- key: taxa:sellimonas_intestinalis
- what it is: a gut bacterium of emerging research interest
- does: ferments carbohydrates within the normal community
- associations: has been proposed as a marker of gut recovery — its return often tracks the community settling back toward balance after a disturbance (emerging association, not proof)
- typical healthy range: low
- confidence: low-to-moderate (emerging)
- citation: Munoz et al. (Sellimonas as a marker of gut homeostasis recovery) [verify in human-gate]
