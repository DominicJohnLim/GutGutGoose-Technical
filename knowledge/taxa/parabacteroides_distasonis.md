# Parabacteroides distasonis
- key: taxa:parabacteroides_distasonis
- what it is: a common gut commensal related to the Bacteroides group
- does: produces succinate and short-chain fatty acids, and helps modify bile acids
- associations: several studies (partly animal models) associate it with metabolic benefits such as improved glucose handling (association, not proof)
- typical healthy range: low single digits
- confidence: moderate
- citation: Wang et al., Cell Reports 2019 (P. distasonis and metabolic benefits, mouse + human data) [verify in human-gate]
