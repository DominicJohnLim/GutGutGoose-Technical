# Fusicatenibacter saccharivorans
- key: taxa:fusicatenibacter_saccharivorans
- what it is: a butyrate-producing commensal of the healthy gut
- does: ferments sugars into butyrate, contributing to the anti-inflammatory short-chain-fatty-acid pool
- associations: reduced levels have been observed in ulcerative colitis (association, not proof); generally regarded as a beneficial member
- typical healthy range: low (under a few percent)
- confidence: moderate
