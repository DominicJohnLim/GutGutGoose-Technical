# Phocaeicola dorei (formerly Bacteroides dorei)
- key: taxa:phocaeicola_dorei
- what it is: a common gut Bacteroidetes, reclassified from Bacteroides to Phocaeicola
- does: breaks down dietary polysaccharides (fiber) and produces short-chain fatty acids; its cell-surface LPS is relatively mild to the immune system
- associations: studied in early-life immune development; some cardiometabolic associations reported (association, not proof)
- typical healthy range: low to moderate
- confidence: moderate
