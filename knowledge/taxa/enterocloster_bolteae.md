# Enterocloster bolteae (formerly Clostridium bolteae)
- key: taxa:enterocloster_bolteae
- what it is: a gut commensal, reclassified from Clostridium to Enterocloster
- does: ferments carbohydrates; part of the normal flora
- associations: studied in the context of dysbiosis; some cohorts report higher levels alongside certain GI or neurodevelopmental conditions (association, weak, NOT proof). Present at low levels in many healthy guts.
- typical healthy range: low
- confidence: low-to-moderate
- citation: general dysbiosis literature [verify in human-gate]
